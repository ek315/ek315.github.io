Project Description

In my senior year of high school in 2015, I got the opportunity to construct an early build of the Prusa I3 printer at NOVA Labs, a local makerspace. I later used my printer to print prosthetic hands using open source designs from E-NABLE, and got a chance to present the results at the Capitol Hill Maker Faire that year.
